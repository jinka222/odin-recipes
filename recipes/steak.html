Pan Seared Steak

Description

Today we'll be making a perfect Pan-Seared Steak. You might just prefer this method over your grill! Perfect crust on the meat, infused with flavors of fresh herbs, this recipe will teleport you straight to a high end steakhouse.

Ingredients

  • Preferred cut of steak
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Thyme
  • A high smoke-point oil

Steps

  1. Pat dry and season room-temperature steak with salt and pepper.

  2. Pour some oil into your pan and bring it to high heat.

  3. When the oil starts to shimmer, drop in your steak and cook for 2-3 minutes before turning.

  4. After 2-3 minutes flip your steak and cook for about 30 seconds before adding a bit of butter, a couple crushed garlic cloves, and fresh Thyme.

  5. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes while basting with butter.

  6. Once your steak is cooked to your desired readiness, remove it from the pan and let rest for 5 minutes before serving!

  7. Enjoy!
